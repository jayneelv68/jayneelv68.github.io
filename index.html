About

More About Me

Jayneel is a final year Computer Engineering student at Institute of Technology, Nirma University.

I have very divergent research and developer profiles. The research spans the communication paradigms- Fog/Edge Computing, Blockchain, Tactile Internet Clouds to make the communication in healthcare and ambient assisted living systems efficient. The developer profile spans BioDesigning software and wearables for Healthcare applications.

So, in a way you can say that the divergent profiles meet at a point- Healthcare.

One spectra of life that has always aspired Jayneel is Healthcare. Every teen has a misguided feeling of knowing himself.

Similiarily I fell for Computer Engineering, while realising through my professional career that I have an equal inclination to know more about the Human Body and Health care systems.

My undergraduate researcherguides have been Dr Sudeep Tanwar and Prof. Pooja Shah.

My Biodesign guide is Dr Prashant Jha.

I am blessed to have worked with Dr Nirav, Dr Sanchez and Dr Shams during my shadowing and internship at Sentinel Healthcare.

I have also been the MozillaNirma Club Captain, 2018.
